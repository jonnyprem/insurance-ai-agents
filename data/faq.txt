Insurance FAQ

1. What does the standard health insurance plan cover?
The standard plan covers preventive care, doctor visits, hospitalization, emergency services, prescription drugs, and approved specialist consultations.

2. What is excluded from coverage?
Cosmetic procedures, experimental treatments unless pre-approved, and services received outside the approved provider network are excluded unless specifically stated in the policy.

3. How do I file a claim?
Members can file a claim by submitting the claim form, itemized invoices, and supporting medical records through the internal claims portal or by email to claims@exampleinsure.local.

4. How long does claims processing take?
Clean claims are typically processed within 7 to 10 business days after all required documents are received.

5. Is pre-authorization required?
Pre-authorization is required for inpatient admissions, major diagnostic imaging, and certain specialty medications.
